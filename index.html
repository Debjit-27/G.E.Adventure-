About us

Green Ecsape Adventure

Green Escape Adventure is a passionate travel company dedicated to unlocking the natural beauty and cultural richness of North East India. Specializing in eco-friendly and immersive travel experiences.

We take you deep into the heart of the Seven Sisters— Assam, Arunachal Pradesh, Manipur, Mizoram, Nagaland, and Tripura . Whether you're seeking lush forests, cascading waterfalls, remote villages, or vibrant local traditions, Green Escape Adventure curates journeys that adventure, authenticity, and sustainability.

Assam | Gateway to North-East

Assam is famed for its tea plantations, the mighty Brahmaputra River, and rich wildlife sanctuaries like Kaziranga. It has a vibrant cultural heritage with Bihu as its major festival and serves as the gateway to Northeast India.

Meghalaya | Abode of Coluds

Known as the “Abode of Clouds,” Meghalaya is famous for its scenic hills, living root bridges, and heavy rainfall. Home to tribes like the Khasis, Jaintias, and Garos, it offers a unique blend of nature and culture.

Next

Arunachal Pradesh | Land of Rising Sun

Arunachal Pradesh, India's easternmost state, is known for its pristine landscapes, monasteries, and diverse tribal communities. The state is a biodiversity hotspot and offers stunning vistas of snow-capped mountains and lush green valleys.

Next

Mizoram | Land of Blue Mountains

Mizoram, meaning "Land of the Hill People," is covered with rolling hills and bamboo forests. It boasts a high literacy rate and vibrant festivals like Chapchar Kut, celebrated by the warm and close-knit Mizo community.

  • Travel packages
Next

Nagaland | Land of Festivals

Nagaland is renowned for its warrior tribes, colorful attire, and the famous Hornbill Festival. Nestled in the Naga Hills, it showcases unique customs, folk music, and traditional crafts, making it culturally rich and distinct.

  • Travel packages
Next

Manipur | Jewel of India

Manipur, often called the “Jewel of India,” is known for its classical dance (Ras Leela), Loktak Lake, and floating islands. It has a diverse cultural heritage and plays a key role in India’s sports and martial arts scene.

  • Travel packages
Next

Tripura | Hidden gem of Northeast

Tripura is rich in history with ancient temples, royal palaces, and tribal heritage. It blends Bengali and tribal cultures and is known for the Neermahal Palace and beautiful landscapes dotted with hills and forests.
